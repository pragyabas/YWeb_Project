Volunteer

Volunteers are an important part of our team and critical to meeting our mission.

Thank you for your interest in volunteering with YWCA Madison.

There are several ways you can volunteer at YWCA Madison and we will do our best to match volunteers interests with our program needs. Please review the current list of volunteer opportunities, fill out our online volunteer application, and you will receive an email letting you know when the next YWCA volunteer opportunity is available. If you have any questions or would like further information, please contact Volunteer Coordinator, Erin Farrar at efarrar@ywcamadison.org

Current Volunteer Opportunities

YWeb Volunteer

Tutoring support-business and personal finance support, ex: how to write a resume, to help students transition into a new working environment
Location: Empowerment Center, 2040 S Park St, Madison WI 53713
Available hours: TBD

Event Volunteer

Support where needed in preparing for, staffing, and general needs of YWCA Madison special events, including Circle, Women of Distinction Awards, and the Racial Justice Summit.
Location: As needed
Available hours: at least 4 hours/month

Clerical Work

On an as-needed basis
Location: 101 E Mifflin Street, Madison, WI 53703
Available hours: Flexible
